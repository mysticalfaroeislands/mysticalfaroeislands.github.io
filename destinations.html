MAP OF THE FAROE ISLANDS


Most of the Faroe Islands are connected either by tunnels or bridges. The road infrastructure in the Faroe Islands is excellent, ensuring a comfortable and safe journey regardless of weather conditions. If you want to plan ahead, these maps could be useful to help you find the best way.
